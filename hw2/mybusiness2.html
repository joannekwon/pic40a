About

Hello! I'm Joanne Kwon and I am the president and founder of Joanne's® Frozen Yogurt. I'm currently attending the University of California, Los Angeles, persuing a B.S. in cognititve science. For the last year, I have been running this company while attending school at UCLA.

Eating frozen yogurt has always been a favorite activity of mine. Living in Los Angeles for the past 3 years, I have had the opportunity to taste many different frozen yogurt flavors. Some were made naturally, others were made artificially. I found that artificially flavored frozen yogurt tended to taste better than frozen yogurt that utilized natural flavors. I wanted to try making my own frozen yogurt that taste good and is naturally made. After months of frusteration and failure, I was able to formulate the perfect mixture of ingredients for all natural frozen yogurt. Finally content with my new frozen yogurt recipe, I found the courage to share them with my friends... and finally with you!
